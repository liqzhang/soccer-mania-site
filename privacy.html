Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Soccer Mania is built to provide soccer news, scores, match information, standings, stats, and match alerts. This policy explains what information the app may use to provide those features.

Information Used by the App

  • Favorite teams, players, and app preferences may be stored on your device.
  • Match alert settings may be sent to the Soccer Mania backend so notifications can be delivered.
  • Push notification tokens may be used to send match alerts to your device.
  • Basic app activity and diagnostics may be processed to improve reliability and understand feature usage.

Third-Party Services

Soccer Mania uses Firebase services for app infrastructure, app integrity, notifications, remote configuration, and related backend features. The app also retrieves soccer data and news from third-party data providers.

External Links

The app may open external articles, ticket pages, or related web pages. Those sites are operated by third parties and have their own privacy practices.

Subscriptions and Purchases

If premium features are enabled, purchases are handled by the app store platform. Soccer Mania may use purchase status to unlock premium features.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact information on the Support page.

Soccer Mania is an independent app and is not affiliated with FIFA, UEFA, any league, club, or governing body.
