Soccer Mania Support

Need help with Soccer Mania? Use this page for common troubleshooting steps and contact information.

Contact

Email: soccermaniaapp@gmail.com

Please include your device model, iOS or Android version, and a short description of the issue.

Common Fixes

  • Pull down to refresh scores, news, standings, or match details.
  • Check that notifications are enabled in your device settings before using match alerts.
  • If live data looks stale, try switching tabs and refreshing again.
  • For external articles or ticket links, make sure your device can open web pages.
